Music Production

I have several years of experience as a musician and as a music producer.

In 2014 I completed a bachelor degree (BA) in music from Liverpool Institute for performing arts (LIPA). My main subjects at LIPA were live- and studio performance and film music/composing.

I have worked as a studio musician, producer and music teacher for many years now, and I am always looking for new and exiting work opportunities. Please get in touch if you want to collaborate or hire me for something!

If you want to see and listen to some examples of my work, just scroll down on the page!
